Greek Koftes

  1. 500g Pork mince
  2. 1 egg
  3. 2 large potatos
  4. 1 large onion
  5. 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • Peel the onion and potatos and finely grate and place in a tea towel
  • Wrap in the tea towel and squeeze out all the excess liquid
  • Place in a large bowl and add the remaining ingredients, mix fully and season with salt and pepper
  • Roll out your koftes into a small sausage shape
  • In a large fying pan add a generous amount of oil
  • Fry your koftes on a medium heat, turning frequently until golden brown
  • Lay out some paper towels and place your cooked koftes on the paper towels to absorb excess oil
