Luscious Lemon Cheesecake

  1. 2 x 300g creamcheese
  2. 300ml double cream
  3. 100g icing sugar
  4. Zest and juice of 3 lemons
  5. 300g shortbread
  6. 50g butter
  • Place the shortbread in a bowl and crush with a cup or blend to a fine consistency
  • Melt the butter in the microwave and add to the shortbread, mix fully and place to a cake tin, pressing down to get an even layer and place in the fridge
  • In a large bowl incorporate the icing sugar and creamcheese, you can do this with either a whisk or spoon
  • Now add the double cream, again mix to form a homogeneous mixture
  • Finally add the zest and juice of the lemons, you will find that this instantly thickens the mixture
  • Take out your cake tin from the fridge add your cheesecake mixture, filling the gaps and smooth on top
  • Refrigerate over night so it fully sets
