Christopher's Signature Lasagna recipe

Indulgent Beef Lasagna

  1. 500g Beef Mince
  2. 500g Pork Mince
  3. 500ml Red Wine
  4. 2 beef stock cubes
  5. 20g Pancetta one packet
  6. 2 gloves of garlic
  7. 1 onion
  8. 1 carrot
  9. 2 sticks of celery
  10. 2 sprigs of thyme
  11. 400g Tinned tomatos x 2
  12. 200ml of milk
  13. Parmesan cheese
  14. Store brought bechamel x 2 400g
  • Preheat oven to 160g degrees Celsius
  • Saute onion, carrot, garlic and celery until they start to break down and the onions become translucent
  • Add in your Pancetta, pork mince and beef mince. Cook until browned ensuring you break up the mince
  • Add in the milk, tinned tomatos and wine, give this a good stir
  • Finally add in the stock cubes and thyme, leave uncovered and gently simmer for 1 hour or until most of the liquid has evaporated
  • Now it's time to assemble your lasanga!
  • Start with a one spoonful of your meat sauce, after this I suggest you divide your meat sauce into 3 dishes
  • Add a layer of pasta sheets, you might need to snap the sheets to fix into the gaps!
  • Add a layer of bechamel sauce
  • Add a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese
  • Repeat 2 more times - Mince - Pasta - bechamel - Parmesan
  • Finish with a layer of pasta and remaining bechamel sauce
  • Place in oven for 1 hour, it should be lovely and bubbling when you take it out
